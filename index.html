Safety is the whole point of Trove. We use 256-bit encryption, turn on 2-factor authentication for every account by default, and pause new withdrawal addresses for review. Create your account, verify your identity once, and add funds by SEPA, card, or Apple Pay. Then choose a coin, check the rate that's locked before you confirm, and buy. Most first purchases take just a few minutes from start to finish.
